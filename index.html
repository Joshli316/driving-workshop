报税Taxes

兼职司机的税务 Taxes for Gig Workers

您是自雇人士 — 公司不扣税，您要自己交 You are self-employed — the app does not withhold taxes
您拿的是 1099-NECYou Get a 1099-NEC
不是 W-2 — 您是「独立承包人」。每年 1 月底 App 寄出 1099 表，列出去年总收入。 Not a W-2 — you're an independent contractor. App sends 1099 in late January showing last year's earnings.
税率约 15.3%~15.3% Self-Employment Tax
联邦自雇税（社保 + 联邦医保） + 联邦所得税 + 加州所得税。建议每赚 $100 留 $25–30 交税。 Self-employment tax (SS + Medicare) + federal income tax + CA state tax. Save $25–30 of every $100 earned.
季度预交税Quarterly Estimated Taxes
如果年收入超过 $1,000，需要每季度交一次：4/15 · 6/15 · 9/15 · 1/15。否则罚款。 If you earn over $1,000/year, pay quarterly: Apr 15 · Jun 15 · Sep 15 · Jan 15. Otherwise penalties.
✓ 可抵扣的支出✓ Deductible Expenses
汽油、修车、保险、手机费、停车费、过路费、洗车。或用每英里 67¢简单算（2024）。 Gas, repairs, insurance, phone, parking, tolls, car wash. Or use 67¢/mile standard deduction (2024).
